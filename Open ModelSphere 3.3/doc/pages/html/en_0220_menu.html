Menu


Open ModelSphere allows you to work with a variety of menus. Firstly, there is the main menu which is always available, although, some commands might be enabled or disabled depending on the selection. The main menu is located at the top of your screen. Secondly, there are the useful pop-up menus that appear simply by right-clicking an object, allowing you to choose from a variety of properties and functions that are specific to this item. Finally, function keys are also available.
